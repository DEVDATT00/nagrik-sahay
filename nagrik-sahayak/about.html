Our Core Objective

Nagrik Sahayak is an AI-powered Smart City civic assistance system designed to simplify and strengthen the way citizens report and track public service issues. We remove language and literacy barriers through voice commands and automated escalation.

#AI_First #InclusiveCity #GovTech

Voice-First

Report issues in any regional dialect. Our LLMs convert speech to structured tickets instantly.

Auto-Escalation

Unresolved issues are automatically bumped to higher authorities until action is verified.

Total Transparency

Unlike traditional systems that stop at registration, we provide voice-based status updates and public heatmaps to keep everyone accountable.
