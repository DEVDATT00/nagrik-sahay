Reporting Issues

Which languages does the AI voice assistant support?
Currently, Nagrik Sahayak supports 12+ major regional languages including Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, Tamil, Bengali, and English. The AI automatically detects your language—you do not need to select it manually before speaking.
What should I do if the AI misinterprets my voice report?
After recording, you will see a live transcription. If there is an error, you can click "Edit Text" to manually correct the report or re-record the audio in a quieter environment.

Tracking & Timeline

How long does it take for a complaint to be resolved?
Resolution time depends on the category. Urgent issues (like water main bursts) have a 24-hour SLA. Routine maintenance (like street light flickering) may take 3-5 business days. You can track real-time progress in your Dashboard.
What is the 'Auto-Escalation' feature?
If a department does not acknowledge or resolve your ticket within the stipulated SLA time, our AI automatically "escalates" the ticket to a senior official or commissioner to ensure accountability.

Privacy & AI Data

Is my voice recording stored permanently?
Your voice recording is used solely to generate the municipal report. Once the AI transcribes and verifies the report, the raw audio is encrypted and archived for 30 days for quality assurance, then permanently deleted.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with any technical issues or urgent civic concerns.
