Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using Nagrik Sahayak, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. This platform is provided by the Municipal Corporation to facilitate civic engagement through AI-assisted reporting. Misuse of the platform, including the filing of false or malicious reports, may result in account suspension or legal action.

2. User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate, geo-tagged data when reporting civic issues. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that occur under your account.

Privacy Policy

3. Data Collection

We collect personal information (name, phone number), location data (GPS coordinates), and audio data (voice recordings) only when you submit a report. This data is essential for the AI to categorize and route your complaint to the correct department.

4. Data Encryption

All data is encrypted using 256-bit AES standards during transmission and at rest. We adhere to the highest governmental standards for data protection and information security.

AI Voice Consent

5. Voice Processing Consent

By using the "Voice Report" feature, you explicitly consent to the recording and processing of your voice by our AI engine. The AI transcribes your regional dialect into a formal English/official municipal report. Original audio is retained for a maximum of 30 days for quality audit purposes before being purged.
